Specialties & Services

Senior Downsizing & Relocation Support Services

Providing respectful, personalized support to older adults navigating the emotional and logistical challenges of downsizing. Services are tailored to preserve dignity and ease the transition to a smaller home or assisted living environment.

• Assistance with sorting and decision-making

• Guidance on items to keep, donate, gift, or sell

• Donation and disposal coordination

• Coordination with family members or caregivers

• Supportive presence during a potentially emotional process

Home Decluttering & Simplification

Offering structured, nonjudgmental assistance to create more functional, manageable living environments. Ideal for clients wishing to simplify without relocating.

• Kitchen, pantry, garage, and storage optimization

• Closet and wardrobe organization

• Write up step-by-step personalized routine documentation

• Home office setup

• Sourcing of containers or labels (if applicable)

• Systems for mail, medications, and paperwork

• Digital file organization

• Accessibility-focused arrangements to reduce physical strain

• Smart-home product recommendations and set up assistance

• Neurodivergent-friendly systems
