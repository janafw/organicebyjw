    Step 3: Book Your Organizing Session

    • Book your session anytime—no consultation required!

    • Hourly rate is $65/hour with a 4-hour minimum per session.
    • For larger or ongoing projects, we’ll plan your schedule together during the consultation or after your first session.

    Step 4: What to Expect During Your Session

    • Each session is hands-on and designed for immediate, meaningful results.
    • You choose how involved you want to be—collaborative or independent.
    • Sessions are tailored to your priorities and goals.
    • You’ll receive professional, respectful, and efficient service.
    • Expect visible progress by the end of every session.

    Step 5: Payment

    • Payment is due at the end of each session.
    • Accepted payment methods: Zelle, Venmo, PayPal, Check, or Cash.
    • Need a receipt for cash or check payments? Just ask—I’m happy to provide one.
