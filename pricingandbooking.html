Pricing & Booking

Rates & Session Structure

  • Rate: $65/hour
  • Session Format: One session = one service day
  • Minimum Session Length: 4 hours
  • Maximum Session Length: 8 hours
  • Booking Options: Choose from 4-, 6-, or 8-hour sessions via Calendly

Each session is a focused, hands-on experience tailored to your goals. Whether we’re organizing a single space or working across multiple areas, the structure is designed to deliver real, lasting results.

Daily Structure

  • One Client Per Session (Day)

I work with only one client per day to ensure your project receives full attention—without rush or interruption.

  • Standard Start Time: 10:00 AM

If you need an earlier start, please include that note when booking. I’ll follow up with confirmation if the request can be accommodated.

  • Availability: Monday through Friday, 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM

Requests outside these hours may be accommodated on a case-by-case basis—particularly for projects with time-sensitive goals or deadlines that require additional flexibility.

Book Your Session

  • New Clients: Book your first session anytime—no Goals Session required.

If you prefer to connect first, you can schedule a complimentary 15-minute consultation to talk through your goals, challenges, and the best approach for your space.

Cancellation Policy

  • If something comes up within 48 hours due to unforeseen circumstances, please reach out as soon as possible so we can arrange an alternative appointment time.
  • There is no fee for cancellations made with timely notice. Advance communication helps keep the schedule running smoothly and respectfully for all clients.
  • Thank you for your consideration. I aim to offer flexible scheduling that supports your current needs and life transitions.
